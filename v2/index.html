The Legend of SciNova

SciNova is Cedar College’s annual science olympiad, a grand tournament where the sharpest young minds gather to test their mettle. For our ninth iteration, we invite you to a realm of medieval fantasy, where science is the ultimate form of alchemy. This year’s theme, Transdisciplinary Exploration, challenges participants to weave together knowledge from disparate fields, forging new discoveries from the crucible of collaboration.

Our Quest

To ignite a passion for science by transcending traditional academic boundaries. We challenge our young scholars not just to solve problems, but to understand the intricate connections between scientific disciplines. Our quest is to foster a new generation of thinkers who see science not as a set of isolated subjects, but as a unified, holistic pursuit of knowledge.

The Nine Realms

The competition unfolds across nine distinct but interconnected modules—the Nine Realms of Knowledge. Each realm presents a unique set of challenges that demand expertise from various scientific fields. Guilds must journey through these realms, combining their skills in physics, biology, chemistry, and more, to unravel the mysteries that lie within and prove their mastery.

Call to Guilds

We summon 40 guilds, each composed of aspiring alchemists and scholars aged 16-19, to embark on this epic journey. This is a call to the curious, the brave, and the ingenious. Assemble your most trusted allies, for the path ahead is fraught with intellectual peril and the promise of untold discovery. Only through teamwork, creativity, and a thirst for knowledge can a guild hope to emerge victorious.
