WHAT IS
SCINNOVA?

Scinnova is Cedar College’s flagship science competition, designed to be more than just an academic challenge. It is an immersive learning experience that brings together 60 teams of budding scientists from across Karachi and Pakistan.

This year’s Scinnova consists of nine modules, each an intellectual arena representing a distinct field of science. Together, they showcase how a deep, interdisciplinary understanding of our world fosters true critical thinking, collaboration, and innovation.

EST. 2017 KARACHI, PK

THE
MODULES

I

ARCANUM

Step into the shoes of a forensic detective. Analyze crime scenes, decrypt hidden messages, and use logic to solve the unsolvable.

II

ILLUMINATIO

Master the elements. From explosive reactions to molecular engineering, this module tests your chemistry prowess.

III

PTOLEMY

The ultimate test of logic and modeling. Solve complex mathematical problems and build models that defy expectations.

IV

PLANCK

Dive into the laws of the universe. From classical mechanics to quantum theory, prove your understanding of physics.

V

ASCLEPIUS

Explore the mysteries of life. Perform dissections, analyze genetic codes, and solve medical mysteries.

VI

REDSHIFT

Look to the stars. Calculate orbital trajectories, identify celestial bodies, and unravel the secrets of the cosmos.

VII

AUTOMATION

Build the future. Design, build, and code robots to perform complex tasks in a high-stakes arena.

VIII

FREUD

Unlock the mind. Analyze human behavior, diagnose psychological conditions, and understand the brain.

IX

QWERTY

The ninth module awaits your discovery. Coming soon.
