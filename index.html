Scinnova is Cedar College’s flagship science competition. It is an immersive learning experience that brings together 60 teams of budding scientists from across Karachi and Pakistan.

This year’s Scinnova consists of nine modules, each an intellectual arena representing a distinct field of science. Together, they showcase how a deep, interdisciplinary understanding of our world fosters true critical thinking.

The Nine Tomes

Arcanum

Arcanum

Using forensic science, data encryption, and logic puzzles, teams tackle scenarios involving cryptographic challenges and crime scene analysis.

Illuminatio

Students engage in hands-on experiments, delving into the composition and transformation of matter, revealing chemical reactions.

Ptolemy's

Mathematics

Ptolemy's Puzzles

Involves complex problem-solving, mathematical modeling, and logical puzzles. Students apply algebra and calculus.

Planck's

Brings theoretical physics to life by guiding students through paradoxes where common sense meets scientific complexity.

Asclepius
